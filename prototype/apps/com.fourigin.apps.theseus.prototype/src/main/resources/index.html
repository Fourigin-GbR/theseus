The webservice is ...

System requests

  • system info:
    /api/system/info
  • system settings:
    /api/system/settings

Product requests

  • product-codes:
    /api/product/_codes
  • product-details of 'forte-mini' and 'aria':
    /api/product/?code=forte-mini,aria
  • product-details for the first 5 products:
    /api/product/_range?limit=5
  • product-details for the products 5 to 8:
    /api/product/_range?limit=3&offset=5

Property requests

  • property-codes:
    /api/property/_codes
  • property-details of 'hour-hand-length' and 'minute-hand-length':
    /api/property/?code=dimension/hour-hand-length,dimension/minute-hand-length
